Mastermind

10 Attempts remaining

The goal of the game is to discover the secret code in 10 attempts or less. A black peg is returned for each guess that is the correct color and also in the correct location. A white peg is returned for each guess that is the correct color, but in the wrong location. The order of the feedback pegs does not necessarily correlate with the secret code. The secret code may or may not have multiples of certain colors, so don't rule that out as a possibility.

Click on the color in the bank to make your selection.

Good Luck!
